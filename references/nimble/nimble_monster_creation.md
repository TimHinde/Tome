# Nimble Monster Creation & Stat Targets

## Level & CR Mapping
A monster's Level is vaguely equivalent to a 5e CR.
`Level = max(1, CR)`
If CR is a fraction (e.g., 1/2, 1/4), calculate it and round or set Level 1. 

## Armor Categories
Armor in Nimble avoids flat numerical thresholds and uses descriptive categories based on original AC:
- **Unarmored:** Converted from 5e AC 13 or lower.
- **Medium Armor:** Converted from 5e AC 14 to 17.
- **Heavy Armor:** Converted from 5e AC 18 or higher.

## HP and Speed
- Hit Points (HP) scale directly from 5e values.
- Speed carries over directly (e.g. "30 ft.").

## Attributes
Nimble 2e does not use standard 5e ability scores (STR/DEX/CON/INT/WIS/CHA) for monster statblocks. These can be dropped entirely during conversion.

## Actions and Resolution
Attack bonuses from 5e are generally ignored during normal play, because in Nimble, attacks hit unless a 1 is rolled, and crit on the maximum value of the damage die. Actions should carry a note about this resolution mechanic.

## Damage Alignment
Damage uses standard 5e dice patterns. Adjust targets slightly if converting a Striker (higher damage) vs Defender (lower damage).
