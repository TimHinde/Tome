# Nimble Core Mechanics

## Action Economy
A typical turn for heroes and monsters consists of 3 actions. Actions can be spent on attacking, moving, defending, or casting spells.
For activities that cost more than 1 action, your actions can be spent over multiple turns in combat.

## Attack Resolution
In Nimble 2e, attack rolls almost always hit.
An attack automatically hits and deals its weapon/spell damage UNLESS you roll a natural 1.
If you roll the maximum number on the damage die, the attack is a Critical Hit and deals maximum damage plus an additional roll of the damage die.

## Saves
Saving throws use standard stats (STR, DEX, INT, WIL). 
Saves are typically rolled to avoid a condition or reduce damage from an area effect.

## Conditions
Conditions apply modifiers such as gaining/losing actions, disadvantage on rolls, or granting advantage to attackers. See nimble_conditions.md for details.
