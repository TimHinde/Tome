# Nimble Damage Types

Nimble 2e relies on standard system damage types (such as Slashing, Piercing, Bludgeoning, Fire, Cold, Lightning, Thunder, Acid, Poison, Necrotic, Radiant, Force, Psychic).

Resistances and Immunities carry over directly from the source system. Any conditions applied as a result of specific damage types (like being poisoned) should be translated to their Nimble condition equivalents.
